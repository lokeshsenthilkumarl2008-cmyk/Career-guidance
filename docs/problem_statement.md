# Problem Statement

## Overview
Many students struggle to choose the right career path due to:
- Lack of awareness
- No personalized guidance
- Confusion about skills and opportunities

## Challenges
- Students don’t know their strengths
- No structured roadmap
- Generic advice (not personalized)

## Impact
- Wrong career choices
- Wasted time and effort
- Lack of confidence

## Goal
Build an AI-powered system that provides personalized career and educational guidance.
